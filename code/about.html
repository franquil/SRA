About

Smart Recruitment Assistant (SRA) is an AI powered tool to help employers to recruit the most suitable staff as efficiently as possible. The scientific evidence based solution is derived from a comprehensive analysis of applicants moods as well as characteristics through voice/facial-recognition/eye detection. And it helps the employees find more suitable positions and build their career development pathways.
